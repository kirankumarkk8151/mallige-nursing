Course Details

Mallige College of Nursing offers a B.Sc Nursing course with a duration of four years, conducted in English and affiliated with RGUHS, catering to a yearly intake of 60 students at the undergraduate level.

The selection process for admission adheres to a strict set of rules and regulations. Candidates are chosen based on their merit and are required to participate in an Entrance Exam, which consists of objective-type (Multiple Choice) questions. Additionally, candidates must meet the essential criteria of medical fitness for the course.
To complete the admission procedure, applicants must submit a completed application form along with photocopies of various documents, including their 10th/SSLC Marks Certificate, 10+2/HSC/PUC Marks Certificate, Transfer Certificate, Conduct Certificate, Migration Certificate, Aadhar Card Copy, and passport-size photographs. Mallige College of Nursing is committed to selecting candidates who meet these criteria for their B.Sc Nursing program.
