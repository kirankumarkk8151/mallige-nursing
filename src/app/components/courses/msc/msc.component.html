Course Details

Mallige College of Nursing offers an M.Sc Nursing course, spanning a duration of 2 years. The annual intake includes specializations in Medical Surgical Nursing, Child Health Nursing, and Community Health Nursing, with each specialization accepting 5 students. This postgraduate program is affiliated with RGUHS.

The selection process is primarily based on merit, and prospective students must also appear for an Entrance Exam, which will be held at a date and venue communicated by the respective department or institution.

Furthermore, To complete the admission procedure, applicants are expected to submit a completed application form along with photocopies of several essential documents, including their 10th/SSLC Marks Certificate, 10+2/HSC/PUC Marks Certificate, Transfer Certificate, Conduct Certificate, Migration Certificate, Aadhar Card Copy, and Degree Marks Sheets.

Specializations

M.Sc. in Medical Surgical Nursing

Medical Surgical Nursing focuses on providing specialized care to patients undergoing surgical procedures and managing complex medical conditions.

M.Sc. in Community Health Nursing

Community Health Nursing involves promoting and maintaining the health of communities and populations and providing preventive and primary care services.

M.Sc. in Paediatric Nursing

Child Health Nursing is dedicated to the healthcare needs of infants, children, and adolescents, emphasizing their growth, development, and well-being.
