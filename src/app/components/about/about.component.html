Welcome to Mallige College of Nursing

In the Year 2003, Mallige Education Foundation and its Trustees established Mallige College of Nursing to meet the increasing demand for skilled and eminent Nursing Professionals. The Foundation with the expertise knowledge and experience in the Medical Science started Mallige College of Nursing with an aim to impart knowledge through quality education in Health Care Sciences and thereby fulfill the health care needs of the society. The foundation which carries a legacy of more than 42 years in the Health Care Service laid a strong and steady foundation for the College and Hospitals played a vital role in molding the Institution to be a top ranked Educational Institution and the first choice among National and International students.

The college motive is to create those competence nurses who can devote their career in a long term basis for solving the health issues of the society and the country and who can be competent and capable Nursing Professionals and achieve success in the Global Market. Mallige College of Nursing helps and supports the students in achieving their goal and develop their natural skills to be responsible, compassionate and professional in the Nursing field as the improvement in students overall discipline brings a great change for the college itself.

This college runs and manages its path by following under the principles and policies of Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences. This college not only promotes the nursing education in the society but also claims to change the environment by fulfilling its needs in the sector of health care and by presenting the awareness of health issues in the society eventually working as the health care donor of the nation. The college and the faculties both believes that a Nurse can change the structure of the society not only by catering services to the needy but also by managing the whole grounding of medical and health care by co-operating with various other health experts and specialists and thus works for laying the strong base for the nursing students as the vital point in the society.
