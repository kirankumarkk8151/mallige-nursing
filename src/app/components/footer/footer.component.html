About

The Foundation with the expertise knowledge and experience in the Medical Science started Mallige College of Nursing with an aim to impart knowledge through quality education in Health Care Sciences and thereby fulfill the health care needs of the society. The foundation which carries a legacy of more than 42 years in the Health Care Service laid a strong and steady foundation for the College and Hospitals played a vital role in molding the Institution to be a top ranked Educational Institution and the first choice among National and International students.
